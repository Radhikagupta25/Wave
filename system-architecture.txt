#Schema
"Don't create a schema because there's a feature. Create a schema because there's a new entity."
-User (already exists due to authentication)=> add:  bio,isOnline,lastSeen,socketId (temporary),blockedUsers
-Conversation=> add: participants: [User],isGroup,groupName,groupAvatar,admins,lastMessage,lastMessageAt,createdBy
-Message=> add:conversation,sender,type,content,attachments,replyTo,reactions,deliveredTo,seenBy,edited,deleted
-Attachment=> add:message,url,publicId,mimeType,filename,size,uploadedBy
-Notification=> add:receiver,sender,type,entityId,isRead,createdAt
